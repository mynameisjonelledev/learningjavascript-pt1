These small, round berries have a smooth, dark blue to purple skin and a sweet, juicy interior. Blueberries are packed with antioxidants, vitamins C and K, and are low in calories, making them a healthy snack. They can be enjoyed fresh, added to cereals, baked into muffins, or blended into smoothies. Their vibrant color and delicious flavor make blueberries a popular choice for adding a burst of sweetness to any dish!
