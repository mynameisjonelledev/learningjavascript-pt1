Broccoli is a nutrient-dense vegetable rich in vitamins C, K, and A, as well as fiber and antioxidants. It belongs to the cruciferous family, which also includes cabbage, cauliflower, and kale. Eating broccoli may support heart health, boost immune function, and reduce inflammation. It can be eaten raw, steamed, or roasted.
