This tropical fruit has smooth, vibrant skin that ranges from green to yellow to red, depending on the variety. Inside, the flesh is golden-yellow, sweet, and juicy, with a large pit in the center. Mangoes are rich in vitamins A and C, as well as antioxidants, making them a nutritious choice. They can be enjoyed fresh, added to smoothies, or used in salsas and desserts. Their delicious flavor and versatility make mangoes a favorite fruit around the world!
